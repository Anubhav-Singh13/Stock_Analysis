## Executive Summary

ION Exchange India is a 60-year-old integrated water treatment company with three segments — Engineering (EPC plants), Chemicals (ion exchange resins), and Consumer Products (water purifiers) — uniquely positioned across the entire water solutions value chain. FY26 has been a sharply negative year: EBITDA margins collapsed from 10.8% to 7.2%, PAT fell 31%, OCF turned negative, and the stock corrected 37% from its peak. The cause is identifiable (West Asia geopolitical disruption hitting GCC contracts, Roha greenfield plant costs, legacy project spillover) but not yet resolved — FY27 is the recovery acid test. At current price, the stock is not cheap on normalized earnings and sits in Weinstein Stage 4. This is a WAIT FOR CONFIRMATION situation.
